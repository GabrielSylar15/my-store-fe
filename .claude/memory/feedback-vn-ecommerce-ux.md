Do not use a dark navbar on GIADE. Vietnamese ecommerce users (Shopee, Tiki, Lazada, Sendo) are trained to expect a **colored or white navbar**, not a dark one. A dark nav feels unfamiliar and creates friction.

**Why:** User explicitly rejected Midnight + Indigo direction after seeing it — "it's so dark." The dark nav was inspired by Western premium tech shops (Keychron, Drop.com), wrong reference for Vietnamese household shoppers.

**How to apply:** Nav bar should always use `bg-primary` (brand color) or white. Reserve dark backgrounds for the footer only, where dark footers are universally accepted.
